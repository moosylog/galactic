## 🌐 Overview

Galactic Keystroke transforms typing practice into a **high-pressure combat system**.

Every incoming word is a threat.  
Every keystroke is a weapon.  
Every mistake is punished.

Your goal is to:
- Survive endless hyperspace sectors  
- Maintain high WPM and accuracy under pressure  
- Eliminate your weakest typing patterns  
- Climb the leaderboard through precision and consistency  

No installs. No setup. Just launch and engage.

---

## 🧠 The Chiral Swarm System

At the core of the game is the **Chiral Grid**—a combat system mapped directly to your physical keyboard.

- Left-hand keys fire **blue plasma**
- Right-hand keys fire **pink plasma**
- Words descend as coordinated attack waves
- Your typing becomes real-time defensive action

This is not passive practice—it is **live-fire training for your muscle memory**.

---

## 🚀 Key Features

* **Adaptive Weakness Targeting:** The system identifies your slowest and most error-prone keys—and forces you to master them under pressure.  
* **Relentless Escalation:** The better you perform, the faster and more aggressive the swarm becomes. There is no plateau.  
* **Boss Fights & Key Abduction:** Every 5 waves, a Boss Ship appears. Mistakes trigger tractor beams that steal your keys mid-game.  
* **Precision Recovery Mechanics:** Captured keys can only be recovered through flawless typing challenges under pressure.  
* **Combo Momentum System:** Perfect accuracy builds multipliers and speed. One mistake can collapse your entire run.  
* **EMP Weapon:** Maintain high performance to charge a full-screen blast (Enter key) that resets overwhelming situations.  
* **Persistent Score Tracking:** Your best runs are stored locally—track improvement over time.  
* **Arcade Feedback Loop:** Neon visuals, impact effects, and responsive input create a fast, competitive feel.

### 📁 Custom Keyboard Setup

Upload your own JSON layout file in the setup menu. The game will automatically:

- Parse your layout  
- Map keys to the on-screen grid  
- Align gameplay with your physical keyboard  

Compatible with MoErgo, ZMK, and other configurators—no manual remapping required.

## 🕹️ How to Play

1. Track incoming swarm words  
2. Type with precision to fire plasma  
3. Destroy enemies before they breach your defense line  
4. Maintain accuracy to preserve your combo  
5. Deploy EMP (Enter key) to clear critical situations  

---

## ⚠️ Enemy Types

* **Swarm Squadrons:** Core attack units that descend in coordinated waves. Must be typed sequentially to eliminate.  
* **Bonus Drones:** Fast-moving targets that reward EMP charge or restore captured keys.  
* **Boss Ship:** A heavy dreadnought appearing every 5 waves. Exploits mistakes and abducts your keys.  
* **Practice Missiles:** Precision challenges launched during key capture—type flawlessly to break shields and recover control.

## 🧬 Core Systems

**The Chiral Grid (You):**  
Your keyboard is your weapon system. Every keystroke reinforces real-world typing mechanics through direct feedback.

**Key Abduction Mechanic:**  
Mistakes during boss encounters result in lost keys—forcing high-pressure recovery gameplay.

**Dual Fire Mode:**  
Rescue captured keys via Bonus Drones to unlock enhanced firing output.

**EMP Weapon:**  
A tactical, screen-clearing blast earned through sustained accuracy and combo performance.
